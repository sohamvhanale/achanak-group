Our History

Founded with devotion and dedication, Shree Ganesh Mandal has been serving society through cultural, educational and social activities for many years. Our Mandal organizes Ganesh Festival celebrations, social awareness programs, blood donation camps, educational support initiatives and community welfare projects.
